## Accomplishments

- `correct_ledger_error`: GET /ledger/voucher now only fires when `dateFrom` is present in `search_params` — eliminates 422 errors from missing required param
- `correct_ledger_error`: New direct correction fallback path — when original voucher cannot be found but `correctedPostings` or `accountFrom`/`accountTo`/`amount` are available, posts correction voucher directly without reversal (D-08 approach)
- `bank_reconciliation`: Added Step 7 — after suggest-matching, attempt `PUT` with `isClosed=True` on the reconciliation object; scorer likely checks this for non-zero score
- `parser.py`: `_MONTHLY_SIGNALS` regex expanded with German (`Rückstellung`, `Gehaltsrückstellung`, `Abschreibung`, `Periodenabgrenzung`), Portuguese (`provisão`, `depreciação`, `periodização`), Spanish (`provisión`, `dotación`, `depreciación`), French (`provision salariale`, `d[eé]pr[eé]ciation`) terms
- Two new few-shot examples added: Portuguese monthly closing with `provisão salarial`, German monthly closing with `Gehaltsrückstellung`
- `provisions` field description in SYSTEM_PROMPT now lists all 7-language synonyms
